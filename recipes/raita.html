Raita

Description

Raita is a side dish in Indian cuisine made of dahi together with raw or cooked vegetables, more seldom fruit, or in the case of boondi raita, with fried droplets of batter made from besan. The closest approximation in western cuisine is a side dish or dip, or a cooked salad.

Ingredients

  • Yogurt
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Coriander Leaves
  • Chat Masala
  • Chilli powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Chop 2 medium size onions into small pieces and add them to a bowl
  2. Grate Â½ of the cucumber using a vegetable grater and add it to the bowl as well. Use the largest shredding holes to grate the cucumber for raita
  3. Chop the tomato, 1 green chili, and a small bunch of coriander leaves. Add everything to the glass bowl as well. (note: make sure to wash the coriander leaves a few times in water and then remove the bottom thick stalk part of while chopping)
  4. Now add 2 cups of thick plain whole milk yogurt to the vegetable mixture in the bowl and season with salt
  5. Now give everything a good mix. (Note: You can also add more yogurt if required and also adjust the salt if required)
  6. Finally, garnish the Raita with cumin powder and paprika. And now it is ready for you to taste and enjoy
