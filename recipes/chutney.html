Chutney

Description

A chutney is a spread typically associated with cuisines of the Indian subcontinent. Chutneys are made in a wide variety of forms, such as a tomato relish, a ground peanut garnish, yogurt or curd, cucumber, spicy coconut, spicy onion or mint dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • Coriander leaves
  • Green chilies
  • Chopped ginger
  • Cumin powder
  • Chaat masala powder
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Rinse 2 cups tightly packed coriander leaves well in water, roughly chop and take them in a small grinder or blender jar.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients
  3. Add 1 or 2 teaspoons water and blend to a smooth chutney. Add more water, if required, for making the chutney. Donâ€™t make it too thin or watery.
